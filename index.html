Welcome to Tic Tac Toe!

Game Types:

- 2 Player: Challenge a friend and showcase your strategic skills.
- VS CPU: Test your abilities against the CPU.
Use the Game Type Selector to choose.

Display Name:

- Want to jazz up your name? While in 2 Player mode, Click on the pencil icon to update your display name.
- Sorry CPU, you're stuck with your default name. Tough luck!

Starting a Game:

- Ready to play? Simply hit the "New Game" button to start.
- The Game will randomly pick someone to start. The selected player's name and shape will be highlighted. It's their turn!

Objective:

The goal is simple: To win, Simply get three of your shapes in a row, vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
